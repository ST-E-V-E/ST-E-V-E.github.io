AI Comic Book

MidJourney

Photoshop

This project used the new capabilities of AI to generate both a narrative and images. My section dealt with a boy and robot growing up together. After the boy’s highschool graduation the robot is abandoned and forgotten. Paneling the comic wasn’t too difficult, although the aspect ratio of some of the generated images aren't wide enough. The most difficult part was finding the right prompts to give us consistent characters and designs through trial and error.
